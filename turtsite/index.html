TROUBLE ON
TURTLE ISLAND

In the year 2045 turtle island faced its biggest threat when the Rainbow Meteor struck down and took out everything except for one thing...the turtles. Eternal Turtles are a collection of 5,555 NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain, featuring over 150 traits with unique shells, hats, clothes & more. Our goal with the turtles is to create a community of people who want to make an impact in the world by helping save the enviroment along with other real-life initiatives.

ROADMAP 1.0

Turtle Dao

Project royalties will be set to 5% and 50% of that (2.5%) will be deposited into a community wallet which will be controlled by the Turtle Dao. Our monumental focus of the project and what we want to achieve involve real life efforts and initiatives for the environment and the Turtle Dao will vote on the decisions that we move forward with as a collective.

Turtle Tools

We recognize that a newcomers time in the space can be overwhelming and we want to help with that. We're doing our part with a collection of web3 tools to help make our holders lives in the space easier. Holders can expect to use tools such as rarity ranks, wallet value checkers, discord bots & more.

Turtle Lore

We will continue to build out the lore and story of Turtle Island. The Evil Eight will play a big part in the story as well as the 5,555 mintable turtles. Users will help us shape the story of Turtle Island by creating lore for their characters that goes along with the story.
